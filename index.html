About me

My name is Cooper Stevens and I grew up in Renton, WA. During my childhood you would always find me around a ball playing some game with my friends. As I got older my love for playing sports never faded but injuries caused me to stop playing. I became interested on how the body worked, so I went to college for physical therapy. I ended up graduating with my bachelors degree and went to work at a nursing home. When the outbreak of COVID-19 spread to my work I began to realize that this was not the line of work I wanted. I've always been interested in how technology works because I get easily frustrated when something works a thousand times and then all of the sudden it stops working.
